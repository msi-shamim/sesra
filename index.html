What it is

SESRA (Lead Finder) is a Chrome extension for sales and business-development teams. It turns the page you're viewing — an Upwork job post, a company website — into a structured lead using AI, optionally enriches it with publicly available detail, and mirrors the result into a Google Sheet you own. Your pipeline stays in one place instead of scattered across browser tabs.

Where your data goes

SESRA runs in your own browser. It writes only to the Google Sheet you connect, and stores your leads locally on your device.

Yours to keep

Leads live in your browser and your own Google Sheet — nowhere else.

Write-only Sheets

Connects to the one sheet you pick. It never reads your other files.

No data resale

Nothing is sold, and nothing is used for advertising.
