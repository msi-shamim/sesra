Privacy Policy

Last updated 7 July 2026 · Increments Inc

How the SESRA (Lead Finder) browser extension handles information when you use it. By installing and using the Extension you agree to the practices described here.

The short version. The Extension runs in your own browser. It sends the content you choose to capture to Google's Gemini AI to extract lead details, and writes the resulting leads to a Google Sheet you own. Your leads are stored locally. We run no server that collects your data, we don't sell data, and we don't use it for advertising.

01 Who we are

The Extension is published by Increments Inc. For any privacy question or request, contact msi.shamim@incrementsinc.com.

02 What the Extension processes

Only when you actively trigger an action, the Extension processes:

  • Page content you capture — the readable text of a page you click Capture on, so a lead can be extracted.
  • Lead details — names, companies, roles, emails, phone numbers, website and social links, and notes.
  • Your settings — targeting preferences, model choice, and the ID of the Google Sheet you connect.
  • Your Gemini API key — a key you provide, used to authenticate your own calls to Google's Gemini API.
  • Google authorization — an OAuth access token that lets the Extension write to the specific Sheet you choose.

03 Where data is stored

  • Locally in your browser. Your captured leads and Gemini API key are stored on your device using the browser's extension storage — not transmitted to any Increments Inc server.
  • Your settings (excluding the API key) may sync across your own signed-in Chrome browsers via Google's built-in extension sync.
  • No backend. Increments Inc operates no server for this Extension and has no access to your leads, keys, or Google data.

04 Third-party services & sharing

To do its job, the Extension sends data to the following, at your direction:

ServiceWhat is sentWhy
Google Gemini APICaptured page text, and lead details being enrichedTo extract and enrich leads with AI, authenticated by your own key.
Google Sheets APIThe lead rows you pushTo write leads into the Sheet you own. Write-only — it never reads your other sheets.
WhatsApp Web (optional)A phone number you choose to check, via your own sessionTo check whether a number is on WhatsApp. No message is ever sent.

Use of these services is also governed by their own terms and privacy policies, including Google's (policies.google.com/privacy). We share your data with no other third parties, and we do not sell it.

05 Google API scopes & Limited Use

The Extension requests the auth/spreadsheets scope solely to create and update the Google Sheet you designate as your lead pipeline. Its use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements: data obtained through Google APIs is used only to provide and improve user-facing features, is not transferred to others except to provide those features, is not used for advertising, and is not read by humans except for security, to comply with law, or with your explicit consent.

06 How long data is kept

Leads and settings stay in your browser's storage until you delete them — via "Clear pipeline data", by removing individual leads, or by uninstalling the Extension. Data written to your Google Sheet stays under your control in your own Google account. Disconnecting Google Sheets in Settings deletes the stored access token from your browser.

07 Your choices

  • Delete all locally stored leads any time from Settings.
  • Disconnect your Google account any time, removing the stored access token.
  • Uninstall the Extension to remove its local storage.
  • Manage or delete data in your Google Sheet directly in Google Sheets.
  • Contact us with any question about the data the Extension processes.

08 Children

The Extension is a business tool and is not directed to children under 16.

09 Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes are reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
